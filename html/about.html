About us

We bring India to your Home just with a click

Our team is all grandmothers, who want to share their food made with love

We cook with imported ingredients and spices from India

As a small traditional Indian restaurant, We open our doors with the aim of sharing 100% authentic Indian cuisine. Family and quality are at the heart of all that we do.

Many of us have moved abroad for different reasons. We want to tell the story of our homeland through food culture and to pass these recipes to our children and the rest of the world. That is why INDIGO KITCHEN is here to connect you, to mother India from the comfort of your home.

At INDIGO KITCHEN you will find original dishes from all parts of India. We serve 4 different authentic dishes per month and deliver them to you.

Our Team consists of retired grandmothers from India living in Germany who love to cook and serve authentic cuisines and share the delicious recipes learned over many years so that the younger generations may acquire their knowledge and to connect with their roots.

We provide you the ability to learn our recipes, with our make it yourself kit which we also deliver. Quality is important to us and so we import all our ingredients to support small businesses in India.
