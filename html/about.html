About us

We bring India to your Home just with 1 click.

Our team is 100% Indian grandmas, willing to share their food love with you.

We cook with only Indian species.

We are a small traditional Indian restaurant, that opened its doors with the aim of sharing the true Indian Food culture with our Families in Germany.

Many of us moved outboard for many different reasons and somehow we haven't been able to taste again the original flavors of our hometown or our children's haven't had the opportunity to do it. That is why INDIGO KITCHEN is here for you, to connect and know your Indian food culture.

Our Team is 100% Indian, we are giving all Indian Grandmas and Grandpas living in Germany the opportunity to share with the younger generations all their knowledge and also a place to feel again at home.

At INDIGO KITCHEN you will find original dishes from all different cities of India. We specialize in 4 dishes only per month because quality is important for us and to have that take time and effort. We import all our ingredients from small businesses in India to support our roots.
